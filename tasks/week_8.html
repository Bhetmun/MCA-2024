Comparing Analysis Methods

What I found when comparing these two methods is that it seems to me like the spectrogram likely reveals more information to us about a piece. For example the waveform clearly is able to display when the music is louder and quieter through the amplitude variations however the spectrogram is able to represent the individual pitches and harmonics within a given time frame. This means that the waveform analysis is quite limited in terms of what we can extract. You can also more clearly see the lengths at which the notes are being held and their intensity whereas a waveform would generalise and simply display the overall volume of the track.
