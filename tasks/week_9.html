Comparing Histograms

What these histograms reveal are the nuances that truly make each of these songs stand out. I chose all three of these songs because similarly to my initial song Die For You, all these songs represent some kind of loss of a lover and grief. I know that the chromagrams reveal the key of the songs, I find interesting that despite all three of these songs sound very different from each other, they are actually in the same or a similar key. Due to my lack of music knowledge I am not certain of what that key is exactly, but after analysing these histograms and relistening to the tracks I began to notice more similarities in the songs despite all of them being created by different artists with nuances in their intention and messages that they wanted to convey.
